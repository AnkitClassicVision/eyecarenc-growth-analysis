Competitive Analysis

Strategic assessment of the optometry landscape within 15 miles of Apex, NC

Competitive Advantage Confirmed

EyeCare For You's 60-minute exam model is 2-3x the industry standard (20-30 minutes). Only one other local practice (Family Eye Wellness in Holly Springs) offers comparable appointment times. This represents a genuine and defensible competitive moat.

SWOT Analysis

Strengths

  • 60-minute exam model (2-3x industry standard)
  • Direct Care positioning - no insurance constraints
  • A+ BBB rating, 10 years in business, 0 complaints
  • Premium independent eyewear without restrictions
  • Family-friendly, community roots since 2015
  • Doctor accessibility during off-hours
  • Patient-first decision making (no corporate overhead)

Weaknesses

  • Cannot compete on cost with VSP/EyeMed patients
  • Limited specialty services (myopia, dry eye)
  • Single location limits geographic reach
  • Price perception without insurance context
  • Smaller marketing budget vs. chains
  • Staffing constraints limit capacity

Opportunities

  • Myopia control market - few certified providers
  • Dry eye specialty - cash-pay aligned
  • Premium eyewear destination positioning
  • HSA/FSA education campaign
  • Corporate wellness partnerships with RTP employers
  • DPC referral network development
  • Specialty contact lenses (scleral, ortho-k)

Threats

  • VSP/EyeMed network dominance
  • Chain marketing budgets (LensCrafters, MyEyeDr)
  • America's Best/Walmart extreme value positioning
  • MyEyeDr private equity rollup of independents
  • Family Eye Wellness as direct-pay competitor
  • Economic sensitivity of direct-pay model
  • Online eyewear disruption (Warby Parker, Zenni)
