Key Findings

Exceptional Demographics

Apex's median household income of $138,442 is nearly 2x the national average. Combined with a 2.64% poverty rate, this indicates high capacity for premium healthcare services.

Strong Support for H-M3

Growing DPC Market

Direct Primary Care market growing at 7-10% CAGR nationally, with NC showing particularly strong employer adoption. HSA-DPC integration (effective 2026) will accelerate growth.

Strong Support for H-M1

Differentiated Experience

60-minute comprehensive exams are 2-3x longer than the industry standard 20-30 minutes. Only one other local practice (Family Eye Wellness) offers comparable time.

Strong Support for H-C1

Insurance Mindset Barrier

Primary growth barrier is the "insurance mindset" - patients default to using vision benefits even when direct care may cost less. 67% of consumers are confused by medical bills.

Key Challenge for H-G3

The Bottom Line

EyeCare For You operates in an exceptionally favorable market with high-income, educated professionals who value quality over price. The 60-minute exam model is a genuine competitive moat, with only one comparable practice in the area. The primary challenge is overcoming the "insurance mindset" through education and transparent pricing communication.

With $1,800 patient LTV, 24:1 LTV:CAC ratio, and $620K+ annual revenue potential at current capacity, the unit economics support aggressive investment in marketing and partnership development. The DPC referral network represents an underexplored growth channel with strong philosophical alignment.
