Strategic Partnerships

DPC referral network and complementary healthcare partnership opportunities

Natural DPC Alignment

EyeCare For You's Direct Care model shares fundamental philosophy with Direct Primary Care (DPC): no insurance billing, extended appointments, transparent pricing, and patient-first care. DPC physicians actively seek specialists who share these values for their patients.

Challenging Evidence

Prior: 0.55 Updated: 0.65
